About Me

I am a Neuroaesthetics and UX Researcher, bridging brain science with user experience. I explore the cognitive and emotional responses to interactive creations, with the goal of building digital interfaces that are both easy to use and beautiful. With a background in Cognitive Neuroscience and practical UX research, I employ mixed methods to translate scientific insights into actionable design recommendations, enhancing user satisfaction and product efficacy.
